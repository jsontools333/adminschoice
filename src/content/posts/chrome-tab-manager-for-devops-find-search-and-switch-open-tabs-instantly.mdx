Most of us face this everyday , a dashboard open somewhere, three tabs for chasing down logs, a PagerDuty alert that just fired, two incident tickets in, a cloud console with configuration and the runbook , and a dozen other tabs you opened to keep an eye of a building up crisis. When the alert escalates and you need that one monitoring tab *right now* , it's nowhere to be found, its is buried in a sea of identical favicons across three browser windows. what you do what everyone does under pressure, go ahead and open a new tab and load the dashboard again. Now you have two. Multiply that across a whole team, a whole day, a whole incident, and the browser becomes its own source of friction. The thing that's supposed to help you move fast quietly slowing you down at the worst possible moment.

Never Lose the Right Tab When It Matters Most, Find the needle *and* clear the haystack.

A new chrome extension QuickTabFinder allows you to find the chrome browser tabs quickly by searching or just scrolling through the alphabartical list . Great to locate & close ducplicate tabs as they are listed under same alphabet.
